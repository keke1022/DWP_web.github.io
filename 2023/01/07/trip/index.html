Trip in Fujian

Our trip to Mount Wuyi

This is our trip to Mount Wuyi (513). We picked up a Xunlongchi(寻龙尺) on the way.

On a plain about the peak of 513, we see many pigeons (we will call them gugu later, because they coo all the time)

In Fuzhou and Jianou

This is an Antai Buliding(安泰楼), I'm wondering whether only students from Antai are allowed.

When we have a stop on Jianou(建瓯), we find a wetland under a bridge. I picked up some flat stones and skipped stones.
